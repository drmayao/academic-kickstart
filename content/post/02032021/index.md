Starting today, Dr. Ma joins the Editorial Board of **IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology** as an Associate Editor serving the area of Vehicular Electronics and Systems.
[IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/RecentIssue.jsp?punumber=25) is dedicated to the publication of peer-reviewed original contributions of research regarding the theory and practice of electrical and electronics technology in vehicles and vehicular systems. More information about the transaction can be found [here](http://www.it.is.tohoku.ac.jp/~tvt/index.html).
